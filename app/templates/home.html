Get rewarded

Covey offers real financial and social incentives to get where you're going without getting into a personal car or truck. Our goal is to help people make the right choice today, so we offer rewards everyone can appreciate. Collaborating with others to take mass transit or ride bicycles offers even greater rewards, and the support and encouragement people can provide to one another is invaluable.

  • Earn rewards every time you use a driving alternative
  • Multiply your rewards by hitting personal milestones
  • Participate in a Flock and boost your earnings
  • Unlock hidden incentives during special events

Make a lasting impact

Climate change is real and here to stay. Unless we make effective changes to our everyday habits, humanity will continue to have a devastating impact on the planet, and future generations will assume the cost. Our challenge today is to make widespread, permanent change that will have measurable effects. Covey is our contribution to that challenge.
